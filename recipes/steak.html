Steak

Description

Mouth-watering juicy rare steak

If you're a lover of meat, you have to try this out.

Ingredients

  • Your favourite cut of steak
  • Cooking oil with a high burn point, such as sunflower oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Season your steak with salt and coarsely ground pepper
  2. Oil to your pan and bring it to a medium-high heat
  3. Once the oil is shimmering and hot enough, add the steak
  4. Cook for 3 minutes on either side, basting the steak frequently in it's own juices and cooking oil
  5. Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes at least
  6. Enjoy your efforts and tuck into that tasty steak
