Omelette

Description

A tasty, quick and easy to make snack

If you're a got a hungry stomach and some eggs in the fridge, give this a go.

Ingredients

  • 4, or more fresh eggs
  • Some butter or your choice of alternative cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper

    • Optional Ingredients

  • Blue cheese
  • A sliced red onion or shallots, fried or raw depending on what you like

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork
  2. Season the beaten eggs with salt and pepper
  3. Heat the butter or oil in a non stick frying pan until the butter has melted and is foaming
  4. Pour the eggs into a pan, moving the pan around to allow the eggs to cover the surface completely
  5. After 20 seconds, use a plastic spatula to scrape a line into the middle from the side of the pan, repeating one or twice more all the runny egg is set
  6. optionally, add any filler you'd like such as blue cheese or sliced onion or ham! Scatter the filling over the top and fold the omelette in half with a spatula
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and enjoy
