Chilli

Description

A hearty, smokey, healthy chilli recipe with succulent meat and tender kidney beans

If you're a lover of paprika, you have to try this out.

Ingredients

  • grams of diced steak
  • a can of chopped tomatoes
  • kidney beans
  • paprika, chilli powder, cumin
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 cube of beef stock

Steps

  1. oil to your pan and bring it to heat gently
  2. Once the oil is ready, add the sliced onion and cook until soft
  3. Add the garlic, red pepper and spices, cook for 5 minutes further
  4. Add the diced steak and cook until browned
  5. Make 400mls of beef stock and add it along with a can of chopped tomatoes
  6. Bring to the boil and stir, add a lid and let simmer for 20 minutes
  7. Add kidney beans, stir and simmer for 10 minutes
  8. Remove from the heat and let the flavours settle with the lid on for 10 minutes<0/li>
  9. Serve up alongside rice and enjoy!
